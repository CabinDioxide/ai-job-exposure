§ 01

核心论断 ——「节点突破后，曲线陡然转折」 The Central Thesis — "After the node, the curve bends sharply"

主流机构沿用任务分解法预测 AI 对就业的渗透，结果普遍滞后真实数据 6–18 个月。根本原因在于：AI 替代就业的轨迹由"关键应用节点"切割——节点之前，模型能力虽已具备，但应用门槛尚存，冲击有限，以辅助形态为主；节点之后，应用瓶颈一旦打通，替代率在 6–12 个月内陡升至 40–93%。 Mainstream institutions use task-decomposition models to forecast AI's labor penetration, and they consistently lag the actual data by 6–18 months. The reason is structural: AI's labor-displacement trajectory is punctuated by "key application nodes." Before the node, capability exists but adoption friction persists — impact is mild, augmentation-led. Once the node breaks the bottleneck open, displacement climbs sharply to 40–93% within 6–12 months.

§ 02

替代机制 ——「Orchestrator + AI 的 12.8 倍乘数」 The Mechanism — "Orchestrator + AI, 12.8× Multiplier"

Anthropic Economic Index 的四个时点数据指向同一图景：少数能够指挥并管理工作流的「AI 调度者」用户，正以约 1:12 的效率，替代原本独立完成同等任务的多数劳动者。需要澄清的是：AEI 数据并不支持「自动化与增强两类完全互换」的简化叙事——「用户下达指令、AI 直接执行」类对话占比在 2025 年 8 月冲高至 38.78% 后回落至 32% 左右。实际发生的并非 AI 全面取代人力，而是劳动结构的整体重组。 Four AEI snapshots converge on a specific picture: a small number of "AI operators" — users who direct and manage workflows — are substituting for many workers who previously did the same tasks independently, at roughly a 1-replaces-12 ratio. One clarification: the AEI data does not support the simplified story that "automation and augmentation just trade places." The share of "user instructs, AI executes" conversations spiked to 38.78% in August 2025, then fell back to around 32%. What is actually happening is not AI replacing humans wholesale, but a restructuring of how labor is organized.
