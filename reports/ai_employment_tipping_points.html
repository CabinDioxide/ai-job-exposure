方法论 Method

本报告追踪了过去三年间五个行业在 AI 产品落地之后实际发生的变化，包括岗位空缺数量的下行轨迹、外包合同的终止情况，以及上市公司管理层在财报电话会议上的相关陈述。 This report tracks the changes that have occurred over the past three years in five industries following the deployment of AI products, including the downward trajectory of job openings, the termination of outsourcing contracts, and statements made by listed-company executives during earnings calls.

研究方法采用直接观察而非建模外推。在每一个具有标志意义的产品发布节点之后，研究者系统检索五个目标行业可获得的招聘数据、合同披露文件、上市公司裁员公告，以及企业高管在公开场合明确归因于 AI 替代的表态。报告中呈现的每一项数据均附有脚注，可回溯至原始文件来源。 The research method employs direct observation rather than model-based extrapolation. After each major product release, the researchers systematically reviewed available hiring data, contract disclosures, listed-company layoff announcements, and statements in which corporate executives publicly attributed workforce reductions to AI substitution across the five target industries. Every data point presented in this report is footnoted to its original source.

§ 01

核心论断 ——「节点突破后，曲线陡然转折」 The Central Thesis — "After the node, the curve bends sharply"

主流机构采用任务分解法对 AI 在就业领域的渗透进行预测，其结果普遍较真实数据滞后 6 至 18 个月。其根本原因在于：AI 对就业的替代轨迹由若干关键应用节点所切割。在节点触发之前，模型能力虽已具备，但应用层面的门槛仍然存在，对就业的冲击有限，多以辅助形态出现；节点触发之后，应用瓶颈得到突破，替代率在 6 至 12 个月内陡升至 40% 至 93% 区间。 Mainstream institutions use task-decomposition methods to forecast AI's penetration of the labor market, and their results consistently lag the actual data by 6 to 18 months. The structural reason is that AI's substitution trajectory in the labor market is segmented by a series of key application nodes. Before a node triggers, model capability is already available but application-layer barriers remain in place, and the impact on employment is limited and mostly takes the form of augmentation. Once the node triggers and the application bottleneck is removed, the substitution rate rises sharply to between 40% and 93% within 6 to 12 months.

INSIGHT 01

三条已实证曲线呈现相同形态：节点触发前 6 至 12 个月接近水平状态，节点触发后 6 至 12 个月陡降至 −20% 至 −35% 区间。该形态并非个别样本的异常值，而是可重复出现的结构性规律。 All three empirical curves exhibit the same shape: they remain near horizontal for the 6 to 12 months preceding the node, then decline sharply to between −20% and −35% during the 6 to 12 months following it. This shape does not represent the anomalous behavior of individual cases but constitutes a structural pattern that recurs across sectors.

INSIGHT 02

2026 年 1 月至 2 月期间，节点 F、G、H 于 60 天内集中触发，构成自 2022 年以来节点密度最高的窗口。资本市场对此作出实时反应——SaaSpocalypse 期间 48 小时内市值蒸发 2850 亿美元，完成了对该轮节点冲击的首次定价。 Between January and February 2026, Nodes F, G, and H triggered in a concentrated cluster within a 60-day window, forming the highest-density node window observed since 2022. Capital markets responded in real time: during the SaaSpocalypse episode, $285B of market capitalization evaporated within 48 hours, constituting the first market-based pricing of this wave of node shocks.

INSIGHT 03

将三条已实证曲线的节点后斜率投影至影视及专业服务两个行业，12 个月的预测区间即可直接由历史结构外推得出，无需假设任何尚未出现的颠覆性技术。 By projecting the post-node slopes of the three empirical curves onto film & video and professional services, the 12-month forecast range can be derived directly from historical structure, without assuming the appearance of any disruptive technology not yet observed.

§ 02

替代机制 ——「Orchestrator + AI 的 12.8 倍乘数」 The Mechanism — "Orchestrator + AI, 12.8× Multiplier"

Anthropic Economic Index 在四个时点采集的数据共同指向同一图景：少数具备工作流指挥与管理能力的 AI 调度者用户，正以约 1:12 的效率，替代原本独立完成同等任务的多数劳动者。需要澄清的是，AEI 数据并不支持「自动化与增强两类完全互换」的简化叙事——「用户下达指令、AI 直接执行」类对话的占比在 2025 年 8 月达到 38.78% 的高点后已回落至 32% 左右。实际发生的并非 AI 对人力的全面取代，而是劳动结构层面的整体重组。 Data collected at four time points by the Anthropic Economic Index converges on a single picture: a small number of "AI operator" users, who possess the ability to direct and manage workflows, are substituting for the larger number of workers who previously completed the same tasks independently, at an efficiency ratio of approximately 1 to 12. One clarification is necessary: the AEI data does not support the simplified narrative that "automation and augmentation are interchangeable categories." The share of conversations classified as "user instructs, AI executes" reached a peak of 38.78% in August 2025 and has since declined to approximately 32%. What is actually occurring is not the wholesale replacement of human labor by AI, but a comprehensive restructuring of how labor is organized.
