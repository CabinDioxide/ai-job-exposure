§ 01

核心论断 ——「节点突破后，曲线陡然转折」 The Central Thesis — "After the node, the curve bends sharply"

主流机构用任务分解法预测 AI 对就业的渗透，结果普遍比真实数据慢 6–18 个月。根本原因是：AI 替代就业的轨迹是由"关键应用节点"切割的——节点之前，能力虽然具备但应用门槛仍在，冲击温和、以辅助为主；节点之后，应用瓶颈一被打通，6–12 个月内替代率就陡升到 40–93%。 Mainstream institutions use task-decomposition models to forecast AI's labor penetration, and they consistently lag the actual data by 6–18 months. The reason is structural: AI's labor-displacement trajectory is punctuated by "key application nodes." Before the node, capability exists but adoption friction persists — impact is mild, augmentation-led. Once the node breaks the bottleneck open, displacement climbs sharply to 40–93% within 6–12 months.

§ 02

替代机制 ——「Orchestrator + AI 12.8x 乘数」 The Mechanism — "Orchestrator + AI, 12.8× Multiplier"

Anthropic Economic Index 的 4 个时点数据指向一个具体画面：少数能命令和管理工作流程的「AI 操盘手」用户，正在以大约 1 个顶 12 个的效率，替代原本独立完成同等任务的多数劳动者。顺便澄清：AEI 数据并不支持"AI 自动化和增强两类完全对调"的简化说法——"用户下指令、AI 直接执行"这类对话占比在 2025 年 8 月冲到 38.78% 后又回落到 32% 左右。真正发生的不是 AI 取代人，而是劳动结构的重排。 Four AEI snapshots converge on a specific picture: a small number of "AI operators" — users who direct and manage workflows — are substituting for many workers who previously did the same tasks independently, at roughly a 1-replaces-12 ratio. One clarification: the AEI data does not support the simplified story that "automation and augmentation just trade places." The share of "user instructs, AI executes" conversations spiked to 38.78% in August 2025, then fell back to around 32%. What is actually happening is not AI replacing humans wholesale, but a restructuring of how labor is organized.

§ 03

客服 ——「AI 替代走完整轮的第一个赛道」 Customer Service — "The first sector to run a full cycle of AI substitution"

A ChatGPT API · GPT-3.5 Turbo · 2023.3.1

客服是目前数据最完整、AI 替代走完一整轮最早的赛道。2023 年 3 月 1 日 ChatGPT API 把 GPT-3.5 Turbo 推上商用，每千 token 0.002 美元——意思是 AI 处理一次客服对话的成本，瞬间降到了人工客服的 1/52。然后 18 个月内全球自由职业客服合同量跌了 16%；3 年后曲线在 -28% 附近停下来不再下探。 Customer service has the most complete data and was the first sector to run a full AI-substitution cycle. On March 1 2023, the ChatGPT API opened GPT-3.5 Turbo for commercial use at $0.002 per 1K tokens — cutting the cost of one AI-handled service exchange to about 1/52 of a human agent overnight. In the 18 months that followed, global freelance CS contract volume dropped 16%; three years on, the curve settled near −28% and stopped falling.
